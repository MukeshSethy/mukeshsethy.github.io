About

I work at the intersection of hardware, controls, and software, with a practical focus on making complex systems reliable in the real world. My background spans robotics development, automotive/EV systems, embedded controllers, and validation.

I have owned ECUs end-to-end, designed I/O and wiring concepts for CAN + sensor/ actuator communication, supported cybersecurity and functional safety assessments, and driven root-cause analysis for component and production issues.

I also build data-driven workflows to monitor systems in the field (signal processing, dashboards/visualization, and GUI tooling) and validate solutions on benches and in test environments.
