About

I work at the intersection of hardware, controls, and software, with a practical focus on making complex systems reliable in the real world. My background spans robotics development, L3 field support, automotive/EV technologies, embedded systems, PCB/electronics design, and validation.

I have owned ECUs end-to-end, designed I/O and wiring harness concepts for CAN, sensor, and actuator communication, supported cybersecurity and functional safety assessments, and driven root-cause analysis for component, production, and multi-site field issues.

I also debug C++/Python firmware, build ROS/Linux workflows on Nvidia Orin Nano, implement RTOS-based firmware on ESP32/STM32, monitor field vehicles, visualize CAN signals, and validate thermal systems on benches and in MIL/SIL/HIL test environments.
