About

I work across hardware, firmware, software, robotics, and system integration, with a practical focus on making complex systems behave reliably in the real world. My experience spans AMR debugging, embedded controllers, mechatronics, and engineering workflows that keep teams aligned.

I am strongest where field issues meet engineering analysis: interpreting robot logs and telemetry, triaging navigation/estimation behavior, and connecting symptoms to concrete fixes across sensors, interfaces, and control boundaries.

I bring structured workflows for validation and documentation - from HIL planning to clear, traceable engineering communication - to move problems from "seen in the field" to "reproduced, understood, and resolved."
